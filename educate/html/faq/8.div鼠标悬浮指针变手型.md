1 鼠标经过div变为手型指针
===

通过css设置"cursor:pointer"可以达到鼠标经过div悬浮时,变为手型指针.示例代码:

```html
<!DOCTYPE html>
<html lang="UTF-8">
<head>
    <title>div鼠标变手</title>
</head>
<body>
<div style="cursor: pointer">
    大屎蛋教程网<br>
    dashidan.com<br>
    div鼠标变手
</div>
</body>
</html>
```
