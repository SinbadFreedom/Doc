<div class="jumbotron">
<p>通过设置`div`的`style`样式`cursor:pointer`可以将鼠标指正经过时设置为手型.</p>
</div>


1 代码示例:
===

```html
<!DOCTYPE html>
<html lang="UTF-8">
<head>
    <title>div鼠标变手</title>
</head>
<body>
<div style="cursor: pointer">
    大屎蛋教程网<br>
    dashidan.com<br>
    div鼠标变手
</div>
</body>
</html>
```
